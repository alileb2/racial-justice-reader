# Racial Justice Reader: Volume 1 – Foundations

**Release Date:** December 2025  
**Tag:** v1.0.0  
**Status:** First stable release

## Overview

This release marks the completion of the inaugural volume of the Racial Justice Reader, a collaboratively built “living syllabus” on **The Ethics of Resistance**. Volume 1 focuses on establishing the historical and conceptual foundations of resistance thought, providing a curated collection of texts, commentary, and discussion questions suitable for classroom use, self‑study, and movement‑building.
